Project: Nest One

After the announcement of the start of construction of the Tashkent City MDC project, the developer of Lot No. 4 "Murad Buildings" held negotiations with companies from Japan, Singapore, America and other countries for two years. The choice was made for the company "Özgüven Mimarlık", which had previously implemented projects in Turkey and in other countries of the world. More than 500 specialists worked on the development of the building project. The design of the skyscraper was developed by the Japanese design organization Nihon Sekken. The study of the soil composition was undertaken by the company Zetas Zemin Teknolojisi A.S. (Turkey), specializing in the study of the soil under the foundation of high-rise structures. The management of the construction process, costs and deadlines, quality control, ecology, safety and labor protection was entrusted to the French company Bureau Veritas.

Project: Cambridge Residence

New premium urban residential complex - Cambridge Residence Commercial! Located in the center of Tashkent, this complex is a modern building with terraces and luxury apartments, providing a comfortable life in a bustling city. The facades of the building are covered with foliage, giving the building a unique look, and the underground parking provides convenient and safe parking for all residents. Cambridge Residence Commercial has a well-developed commercial infrastructure that allows residents to work, relax and shop without leaving the building. The complex offers a wide range of additional services, including concierge service, which provides a high level of comfort for all residents.
